QUALITY POLICY STATEMENT

Hezky Medical and Reproductive Health Limited (HMRH) is committed to advancing medically assisted reproductive medicine and laboratory services through in vitro fertilisation (IVF) technologies, equipment supplies, and specialised consulting. We help clinics by setting up labs, providing supplies, and offering technical support, which allows them to provide safe, accurate, and high-quality care for patients while enhancing their ability to offer modern andrology and fertility services.

Our quality management system (QMS) is designed to meet all requirements stipulated in ISO 9001:2015, including compliance with all applicable ethical, statutory, and regulatory requirements. Our commitment lies in continuously improving our QMS to ensure the satisfaction of patients, healthcare providers, partners, and other stakeholders.

This quality policy provides the framework for setting and reviewing quality objectives and drives our pursuit of continuous improvement. It is communicated to all staff and partners, embedded in our daily operations, and periodically reviewed to ensure its suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness.

Our core commitments are:

  • Advancing medically assisted reproductive medicine – delivering innovative and reliable IVF laboratory solutions.
  • Operating an ISO 9001:2015 compliant Quality Management System – ensuring consistency, traceability, and excellence.
  • Meeting ethical, statutory and regulatory requirements – upholding the highest standards of integrity and compliance.
  • Continual improvement – proactively enhancing our processes, products, and services.
  • Customer and stakeholder satisfaction – building lasting partnerships through trust and performance.
  • Periodic review of the Quality Management System – ensuring ongoing relevance and effectiveness.

This policy is the cornerstone of our operations and is available to all interested parties upon request.
